About Us

Our company, Uvents, is an LLC, founded in 2014 by Sharang Ranjan and a web application company, Zerobyone. The service offered by our company is a free app, downloadable by IOS and Android users. The app offers an interactive platform where students can log into the app, create events, view events on and off campus and be able to view friend’s activity too. The opportunity in front of us lies in the untapped market for a massive amount of potential users, with over 60,000 students at Arizona State University..
